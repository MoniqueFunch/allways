OUR MISSION

We are ALLways. The first gender neutral menstrual care brand. We strive to be as inclusive as possible in order to support the LGBTQ+ community.

Periods transcend binaries and unfortunately this excludes a group of people. A group of people who also have period cycles, but does not necessarily identify with being a woman.

We stand up for our nonbinary and trandgender family. We acknowledge your rights and see the issues that you face, not just when buying tampons, but in all aspects of life. Therefore 10% of our profit goes til support the LGBTQ+community and queer bussinesses.

We encourage all of you, no matter if you are a cisgendered woman or transgendered man, to share your bloodstory. Join the movement and share your photos under #BleedBeyondTheBinary in order to show that you can #BleedALLways.
